PLAYER MOVE
***********
On their turn, a player can mark any open square.
A player can drop a piece in any open column on their turn.
On their turn, a player can move any one piece.
A player can make four moves, moving any one piece each time.

PIECE MOVES
***********
A bishop can move in open diagonals.
A rook can move along open rows or columns.
Pawns can move forward one square.
